# candlestick.sh

## overview

candlestick is a stock tracking app for people who want one clear place to follow the market.

## what it does

the app opens on a dashboard with market widgets, charts, technical views, and data. you can search for a stock, open its page, and see its chart, company profile, fundamentals, and technical analysis in one view.

## main flow

search -> open a stock -> save it -> watch it later.

## watchlist and alerts

your watchlist keeps important stocks on a single screen, so you do not need to search for the same names again. alerts let you set a price target and get an email when the stock crosses it. settings let you choose what appears on the dashboard and manage market digest emails.

## why it exists

candlestick is built for focused use. it keeps the screen useful, and not crowded. it helps you check a stock, compare them, and come back to the symbols you care about.

accounts let each person keep their own saved list and dashboard setup.

it is just a clean workspace for watching stocks and staying aware.
